About Tigers

Tigers are the largest and most powerful cats in the world. They are known for their striking appearance and impressive characteristics.Among the largest species of cats in the world, tigers are powerful hunters with sharp teeth, strong jaws and agile bodies. They range across Asia from Russia all the way to Sumatra and mainland Southeast Asia. Researchers still have much to learn about these beautiful, endangered cats.

Tiger Characteristics

Tigers are characterized by:

Distinctive orange fur with black stripes

Sharp claws and powerful jaws

Excellent swimmers

Nocturnal and solitary behavior
